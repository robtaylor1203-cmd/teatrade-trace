    Methodology

    All emissions are calculated using the GHG-Protocol Scope 3 standard, with categories 1 (Purchased Goods), 4 (Upstream Transport), 9 (Downstream Transport) and 11 (Use of Sold Products) in scope. Activity factors are sourced from DEFRA 2025 and IMO MEPC.337(76).

    co2(batch) = Σ(
  cultivation     · estate_factor[lat,lng,grade]      // primary data
  + factory_kWh   · grid_factor[country,year]
  + inland_road   · DEFRA(HGV, payload, miles)
  + sea_freight   · IMO_EEDI(vessel, payload, route)
  + uk_warehouse  · scope2_avg
)

    All hashes are anchored on-chain at block #18,402,119. Independent assurance by EcoCert (ISAE 3000).
