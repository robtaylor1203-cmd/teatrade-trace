    Methodology & conversion factors

    All emissions are calculated using primary activity data captured at ledger event time, multiplied by the published DEFRA 2026 and IMO MEPC.337(76) factors.

    co2(lot) = Σ(
  cultivation     · estate_factor[country, grade, energy mix]    // primary
  + factory_kWh   · grid_factor[country, year]                   // primary
  + inland_road   · DEFRA 2026 (HGV, payload, miles)
  + sea_freight   · IMO_EEDI(vessel, payload, route)
  + bulk_pack     · material_factor[sack | chest | IBC]
  + blend_pack    · sku_factor[format, material]
  + retail_dist   · DEFRA 2026 (HGV last-mile)
  + warehouse     · scope2_avg
)
